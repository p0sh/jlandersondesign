Designer + Researcher + Librarian

I apply design thinking not just to products, but to teams, systems, and culture. Because of my natural empathy, I excel at understanding the user as well as fostering team collaboration. I have a M.S. in Library and Information Science, which trained me to assess user needs and facilitate information delivery in many contexts, including individual user experience, group design work, and organizational growth.

I enjoy working with a team of superheroes with whom I can build beautiful ways to access information.
